COUNSELING

The therapeutic process can take many shapes and forms.
I work with individuals, couples, children, and families.

Together we examine your unique experiences and ways of coping while thoughtfully linking these to earlier times in life. My orientation stems from current research on attachment and training in contemporary psychoanalysis.

You are entitled to have more fulfilling, honest, and joyful relationships; to feel accomplished and successful in your work in the world, and to more easily and comfortably be who you are.

I will work with you one on one or as a couple or family. Sessions take place anywhere from once a week to three times a week. The sessions are 50 minutes in length.

When working with children individually, initially the entire family or parents alone meet and then again at about every fifth session. I believe that the familyâ€™s involvement and input is integral to the therapeutic process and success for all involved.

Depression, anxiety, life transitions, irritability, marital difficulties, child rearing challenges, and family stress are a few of the issues my clients bring into therapy.
